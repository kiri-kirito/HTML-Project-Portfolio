The Best Animes According To
Kirito

Top 3 Animes Of All Time:

1. One Piece

One Piece is an epic tale of adventure, friendship, and the pursuit of dreams. Follow Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they sail the Grand Line in search of the ultimate treasure, the One Piece.

2. Naruto

Naruto is a story of perseverance, friendship, and the journey to become the strongest ninja. Follow Naruto Uzumaki as he trains to become Hokage and protect his village.

3. Bleach

Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper. He battles evil spirits and protects the living world from supernatural threats.
